About Us




IGMASS is a web portal for students of IGDTUW university. It is specially designed for the girls of this university. Students can see their results and their progress on this. They would get recent updates.
   
IGMASS team has ananlysed their result and have shown ranks, marks distribution and aggregate percentage of students via a scripting programming used. The team has made an effort to show the accurate and correct analysis but
can't assure/guarantee for the same. IGMASS gives a portal for previous year question papers according to the course of the student. Analysis is done on the basis of enrolment number and name of the student. For further scope, the team is working on notes and attendance provided to the students.
